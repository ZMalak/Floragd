Variety of Cars, Bikes, Aeroplanes, Helicopters etc in model as well as working mode, with and without remote control.

Soft toys for all occasion and age group. Teddy bear, Animal soft toys, Cartoon character etc in various sizes are available.

Wooden toys especially made for educational purpose are available. These are unbreakable. Child can hold firmly. Easy way to learn Alphabets, Colors, Shapes, Counting etc. Also supplied to schools.

Candle Making, Sequine Art, Nail Art, Origami and Clay Art are just a few of them. Here also have a few activity games like Mechanix and Transformers for boys.
